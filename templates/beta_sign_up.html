About ToshiTicket

ToshiTicket is an open platform to buy and sell tickets on the Bitcoin Blockchain. No longer do music fans have to worry about buying illegitimate tickets on third party websites.

By utilizing Colored Coins and the Open Assets Protocol we make it easy and secure to create and issue tickets on the Bitcoin Blockchain. Learn more about Colored Coins here. Colored Coins. Learn more about the Open Assets Protocol. Open Assets Protocol.

All public data is stored on the Bitcoin Blockchain.
